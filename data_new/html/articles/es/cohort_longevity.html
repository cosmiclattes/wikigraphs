Article Cohort Longevity

What is this graph?

This graph shows the retention in edit activity of articles in an article cohort over its lifetime.

How do I read the graph?

  • When an article has edits >= 5/month by logged in editors the article is considered active.
  • Articles are grouped by the month in which they were created.
  • X-axis (time since the start of the wiki in months)
  • Y-axis (editor cohorts)
  • Each row shows the longevity/retention of an article cohort over time.
  • Each data point gives the percentage of article which were active in a given month (column) from the cohort(row).
  • The demoninator in each row, is the total number of articles that were active in a given cohort in its lifetime.
  • The brush lets you filter the percentage values.
