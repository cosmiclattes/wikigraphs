Editor Monthly Activity - Percentage

What is this graph?

This graph shows the monthly edit activity split by the % contribution from each editor cohort.

How do I read the graph?

  • When an editor has edits >= 5/month the editor is considered active.
  • Editors are grouped by the month in which they mzde their first edit.
  • X-axis (time since the start of the wiki in months)
  • Y-axis (% contribution split by the cohorts of active editors in the month.)
  • Each data point gives the %contribution of a editor cohort in a given month.
  • The brush lets you filter the graph by the age of a cohort in a month. For ex, to see the contribution of only cohorts of age 1 month in every month we select 1-2 in the brush. This lets us see the contribution of the active editors created in a month to the total active editors in the given month in %.
