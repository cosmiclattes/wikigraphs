Editor Sessions(Count) vs Articles

What is this graph?

This graph shows the relationship between the number of months an editor has edited in and the number of articles the editor has edited. On the Y-axis we arrange editors by the number of months they have edited in and on the X-axis we have the number of articles. The number of articles are buckets which are multiples of 5.

How do I read the graph?

  • X-axis - Number of editors, buckets in multiples of 5.
  • Y-axis - Number of months the editor has edited in.
  • Each data point is the percentage of editors that have edited(no of articles - X-axis) from the total number of editors who have edited in (no of months Y-axis)
  • The brush lets you filter by the percentage values of the data points.
